    About HR Workways

    This website has 3 major functionalities, Employee management, Department management and Visitor management.
    In this world of growing technologies everything has been computerized. With large number of work opportunities the Human workforce has increased.
    Thus there is a need of a system which can handle the data of such a large number of Employees in an organization.
    This project simplifies the task of maintaining records because of its user friendly nature.
       
    Employee Management component facilitates adding new employee details, view existing employee details and deleting the employee details.
    Department Management component helps assign a particular department to an employee, edit the details and delete them.
    Visitor management component basically consists of details of visitors on a daily basis.
